Travel precautions

Dear Customer
For your safety concerns, please confirm adherence to the preventive measures against Corona Virus (COVID-19) before starting to book for travel on our flights:

  • Allowing entry to the station will only be for passengers who wear masks and have tickets or wish to book for travel.
  • Passengers will be examined at the entrance to the station, and those who do not wear a mask, show symptoms of infection, or have a temperature of more than 38 degrees Celsius, will be denied entry.
  • To avoid infection, our guests must adhere to the designated seat throughout the flight.
  • In order to preserve public health, a safe distance (one and a half to two meters) must be left at the charging counter or issuing tickets and when getting on or off the bus.
  • Terms and procedures are subject to continuous updating and without prior notice.
  • Travelers should check the latest information about the conditions and instructions required for travel from official and authorized sources before traveling.

Necessary cookies

Necessary cookies are crucial for the basic functions of the website and the website will not work in its intended way without them.
Cookies required for all website functionality needed by site visitors. This includes authentication, language selection, etc. From the visitor's perspective, this means "Allow only cookies that I may need, but do not track me".

Analytical cookies

All cookies that are not assigned to an explicit level are considered to be cookies identifying the visitor, i.e. user tracking cookies, which are not really needed, but are useful for the site owner if using marketing features.

Profiling cookies

Allows all cookies, no matter what their level is. From the site visitor's perspective this means "Allow all cookies now and in the future".
