How can I see ticket prices and trip schedules?

You can view trip schedules and ticket prices by visiting our website www.saptco.com.sa, calling the number 920000877 or visiting our social media accounts, Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.

What documents are required to travel on SAPTCO trips?

The traveler should get one of the following the original: copy of the national ID, residence ID, or passport ID.

What payment methods are available during the booking via the website?

When booking through the website, you can pay via credit cards ( Visa - Master Card ) or via Sadad service.

Can I book immediately without having to book in advance?

You can head to stations or agents to book trips, but you need to head an hour beforehand to book national trips and two hours beforehand for international trips, if available.

Where do I get the ticket after booking and paying via the website?

You will be receiving a text message on the mobile phone with the ticket link. Please make sure to add the correct number during the booking. In addition, you can get the ticket through the website or by the registered e-mail during the booking process, where you will receive an email on your ticket.

Can I board the bus if I book my ticket at 12AM?

The bus can only be boarded by posting the ticket from the dealer or the main station. If the trip is closed, the driver is not entitled to transport the travelers even when the ticket is presented; however, in case the flight is not closed the customer can broad the bus.

What if something went wrong while booking via the website?

If there was an issue during the booking via the website, please contact customers service at 920000877 to report the problem.

What is the procedure in case the ticket is modified or upgraded?

Trip schedule adjustment / Trip schedule adjustment is possible in one of the following ways:

  • Visiting our website and editing the date through the "Reservation Management" icon.
  • Visiting one of our main widespread stations across cities.

Editing destination / you can edit the destination by:

Heading to one of our near stations. If the sum of the ticket after the adjustment equals its value before the amendment, there will be no additional fee, but if the sum after the adjustment is higher, the price difference will be paid.

How can I cancel my reservation?

You can cancel your reservation through one of the following methods:
- Through the website if the reservation is online and you pay with credit cards (Visa - MasterCard).
- If the reservation was from stations or agents and you pay in cash, you can head to one of the main stations.
- When canceling and requesting a refund of the ticket, 35% of the ticket value will be deducted from the cancellation fee.

Are there any additional costs in case of traveling with more luggage than what was booked?

You will have to pay an additional cost if your luggage exceeds the allowable weight by type of trip (inter-city, international, GCC).

What should I do in case my luggage was damaged or missed?

If your luggage was damaged or missed when arriving at your destination, the missing luggage must be reported to the station specialists immediately or utmost 15 minutes after the arrival of the trip. According to the company's policy, the company is not responsible for the loss of the valuable luggage, and its liability in the event of loss of baggage registered with the company's systems is determined by compensation of a maximum of 350 SR per piece after the completion of the legal procedures and the availability of the poster of the missing piece.

Is there compensation for personal losses?

According to the company's policy, there is no compensation for personal losses inside the bus, and it is the responsibility of the customer and is only searched for.

Are there special discounts for students?

SAPTCO provides special offers for students. All the details of the offers can be found by visiting the Ongoing  Offers page 

Are there special discounts for retirees?

SAPTCO provides offers for retirees and their families. You can view all the details of the offers by visiting the Ongoing  Offers page 

Does SAPTCO provide services or offers for people with disabilities?

SAPTCO provides offers for people with disabilities, and all the details of the offers can be found by visiting the Ongoing  Offers page 

Does SAPTCO offer special discounts for the families of martyrs?

SAPTCO offers discounts to the families of martyrs, and all details can be found by visiting the Ongoing  Offers page 

How can I use the Promo code?

Enter the Promo code you have in the "Promo Code" field on the site when searching for your trip, and the discount value will be calculated automatically.

Are people with disabilities served faster when visiting the main stations?

Special selling points have been allocated to serve people with disabilities to facilitate their procedures and finish them faster.

Does the driver continue the trip in the case of harsh climatic conditions?

The estimation is based on the condition and the severity of the situation.

Will I be notified if the trip is delayed or cancelled?

If the trip is delayed or cancelled you will be receiving a text message on the mobile phone registered with the reservation, please make sure to add the correct number during the reservation.

What services will I get in case of canceling my SAPTCO trip?

The ticket will be transferred to the nearest alternate trip if available and adjusted the date if no suitable trip is available.

 

Are pets allowed on SAPTCO trips?

According to the company's policy, pets are not allowed on our trips.

Are meals offered on economy class?

According to the company's policy, meals are not provided on economy class.

Are all buses supplied with a refrigerator?

Yes, all buses are supplied with a refrigerator.

Can I smoke on the bus?

Smoking is not allowed on all SAPTCO trips.

Is there an emergency first aid kit inside the bus?

All SAPTCO buses are supplied with basic safety means, such as (fire extinguisher, spare tire, emergency exits, and first aid kit).

Is maintenance done on the bus before it departs on the trip?

All SAPTCO buses are subject to scheduled and periodic maintenance, as well as a dedicated inspection before any trip.

Is it possible to give the driver a package or transfer papers in order to deliver them?

We apologize to you. It's unauthorized transferring packages or papers and hope you heading to the authorized companies.

What is VAT?

Value Added Tax (VAT) is an indirect tax that applies to goods and services located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia except as expressly exempted.

What is the percentage of goods and services included in the tax?

The prescribed percentage equal to 15% applies to the goods and services included in the tax.

Shall VAT be applied to SAPTCO's services?

Yes, VAT shall apply to the following SAPTCO services:

  • Domestic transport services
  • Inter-city transportation services (only in KSA)
  • Contracts and leasing services
  • Cargo Services

What are SAPTCO services excluded from VAT?

SAPTCO services excluded from VAT include international transport services as follows:

  • International transport services
  • Contracts and international leasing services
  • International cargo services

Shall I have a refund of the tax I paid in case the ticket is refunded?

If your ticket is refundable, you shall be refunded 65% of the ticket and 65% of the tax paid.

What is the procedure in case the ticket is changed or upgraded?

If the ticket is modified at the same value and without the amendment fee, there will be no additional amount. If the ticket is upgraded by a higher value, you will pay the difference in price for the two tickets plus a 5% tax on the difference value only.

Does the tax include effective contracts signed with SAPTCO customers before January 1st, 2017?

VAT includes contracts if they were effective on January 1st, 2018 and the term shall be subject after January 1st, 2018, for the tax only.

Shall indirect international trips be subject to VAT?

Yes, part of the international indirect trips within Saudi Arabia shall be subject to VAT, for example, trips to Egypt.

What is a public transport service?

SAPTCO offers public transport via a route network covering the Kingdom's main cities (Riyadh – Jeddah – Madinah) from 5:30 am to 11:30pm. Soon, SAPTCO will provide public transport service through a network covering Dammam, Qatif and Taif from 5:30 am to 11:30 pm.

What are the working hours of the inter-city transportation service?

The service starts from 5:30 am to 11:30pm daily.

What is the price of the inter-city transportation service?

The service price is 3.45 SR per a trip.

What is the price of a plastic card for inter-city transportation?

The price of the plastic card is 10 SR, and it is without credit. In addition, the balance can be filled or recharged through the bus driver, through the sales officer, or through the application.

Can I benefit from the card in all the cities of the Kingdom?

Currently you can only use the inter-city transportation card in Riyadh and Jeddah.

What is the validity of the inter-city transportation card?

The card has shelf life of one year from the last use.

Can I refund the inter-city transportation card?

Sorry, the sold card is non-refundable.

What is Urban City Transport?

It is the service of transporting passengers on the road that connecting cities and villages of the Kingdom. Its importance is due to the wide area of the Kingdom and its outskirts. It is characterized by its low cost, improvement and development of its means and the level of its quality, as well as easy access to all cities and villages of the Kingdom where flights depart daily from (13) stations and passes. With (130) agents covering all parts of the Kingdom in all directions, it also links about (387) cities and villages with a regular and coordinated network of lines through approximately (450) trips per day. Intercity transport lines are the main support of encouraging domestic tourism and developing and consolidating the culture of travel in the Kingdom.

How long is the ticket valid?

The ticket is valid for use or refund until 15-10-2023, noting that in the event of a refund, a cancellation fee will be calculated at 35% of the ticket value.

What is the allowed luggage weigh?

The passenger on domestic flights is allowed to weigh (75) kg for the full ticket (adults), and (35) kg for half the ticket (for children from 2 years to 12 years old) and an amount of one riyal is added for each additional kilo of the allowed weight.

The size of the luggage pieces must be appropriate to the luggage storage space in the bus according to the company's policy and according to the following dimensions (50 cm width, 90 cm length, 40 cm height).

What are the agents’ working hours?

The working hours of agents’ offices differ from one city to another. You can view the working days and hours by visiting our website www.saptco.com.sa or by calling the unified number 920000877.

How long does the bus stop at the Miqat?

The time limit for parking in the time to honor passengers is from (20) to (30) minutes maximum depending on the solvency of the bus.

What is an International Trip service?

SAPTCO offers the passenger an opportunity to travel internationally through modern, equipped and safe buses. Trips start from all cities of the Kingdom and reach most of the neighbouring Gulf and Arab countries: UAE, Bahrain and Egypt.

How much luggage weight is allowed in International Trips?

International flights (Gulf Cooperation Council countries):

Passengers on international flights (GCC countries) are allowed to carry 50 kg for the full ticket (adults) and 25 kg for the half ticket (for children from 2 years to 12 years old)
In addition, an amount of 2 riyals is added for each additional kilogram of the permitted weight, and the weight of one piece must not exceed 25 kilograms.

International flights to the Arab Republic of Egypt:

Flights to the Arab Republic of Egypt The customer is allowed to weigh 100 kg for the full ticket and 50 kg for half the ticket (or as approved by the company) and an amount of 3 riyals is added for each additional kilo for the permissible weight, and the customer is also allowed to carry luggage on board SAPTCO buses by one bag not exceeding 25 kilos as a maximum, and below that, an amount of 3 riyals is added for each excess kilo.

The size of the luggage pieces must be suitable for the luggage storage space on the bus according to the company’s policy and according to the following dimensions (50 cm width, 90 cm length, 40 cm height).

What is the VIP express service?

They are luxurious and distinctive trips from SAPTCO that are direct trips with no transit stops, in which special services are provided to passengers inside and outside the bus, bearing in mind that the duration and distance of these trips range from short to medium.

What are the available VIP services?

- Direct flights (non-stop)
- Snacks
- Hot drinks
- Internet Wi-Fi
- Electric charging ports
- Comfortable seats with screens (entertainment content, live broadcast, radio, movies, Islamic, radio, children's programs) in Arabic and English.

On what lines do the VIP service operate?

- Riyadh - Khobar - Riyadh
- Medina - Mecca - Medina
- Medina - Jeddah - Medina
- Jeddah - Yanbu - Jeddah

What is the private bus rental service?

It is a bus rental service for direct beneficiaries or organizers of special programs, transporting local and foreign pilgrims and visitors between the Umrah and shrine areas in both Makkah and Madinah, and rental for private trips and for tourist and archaeological areas within cities and between cities, and the rental system by day or hour.

Can I benefit from renting buses inside and outside the Kingdom?

Yes, you can benefit from the service inside and outside the Kingdom.

What is the International rental service?

A bus rental services for pilgrims, Umrah performers, tourists and private trips to countries that SAPTCO services either via agents or tourists offices.

What is the prayer service?

A service that consistently transfers worshipers from hostels and hotels to the Holy Mosque of Makkah and vice versa.

What is the sacred site service?

A service that transports pilgrims between Pilgrimage locations (AlHaram – Mina – Arafat – Muzdalifah). SAPTCO takes an important and influential role in transporting pilgrims at all stages to facilitate their transportation and facilitate the performance of their rituals, where they are transported with an equipped fleet of all types of buses designated for these services and according to the wishes of customers.

What do the services of General Syndicate of Automobiles mean?

The company plays an important and influential role in the activities of transporting pilgrims at all stages to facilitate their movement and facilitate the performance of their rituals. SAPTCO has achieved through its membership in the General Syndicate of Automobiles the optimal participation in the transport of pilgrims from airports and between the cities of Hajj (Mecca - Jeddah - Medina) before and after the escalation of the sacred locations as well as  transporting pilgrims between the Holy Sites (Mona - Arafat - Muzdalifa) were they are transported by an equipped fleet of different types of buses dedicated to these services.

What are Umrah Services?

The company plays an important and influential role in the Umrah activity in terms of providing visas to the company's external agent and receiving and leaving Umrah pilgrims through airports and transporting them between Umrah and Visit’s cities (Jeddah - Makkah - Medina) in all its stages to facilitate their transportation and facilitate the performance of their rituals

What is the Ongoing Contracts Service?

Based on the continuous pursuit of the wishes of several segments of companies, institutions, schools, and universities according to annual contracts for the services of transporting employees and workers from their place of residence and to their places of work and return and a transfer service for students and trainees from their residence to schools, universities, institutes, training centers and return, this service stands out in facilitating the process of mobility easily and smoothly.
