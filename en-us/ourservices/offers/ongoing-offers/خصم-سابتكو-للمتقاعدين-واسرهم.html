Retirees and their families

08-04-2022

Offer details:

 
 
  • 15% discount for PPP employees and their families on VIP Express service trips on the website and app, using code: PP-001.
  • 25% discount for PA employees and their family on economic trips on the website and application, using code: PP-002.
  • 10% discount for PA employees and their family on VIP Express service flights at both stations and agents, using code: PP-003.
  • 20% discount for PA employees and their families on economy trips at both terminals and agents, using code: PP-004
Terms & Conditions:
 
1- The offer is applicable to retirees and their families (mother, father, wife and children) and their beneficiaries (who are entitled to a pension after the death of the retiree).
2- One of the following documents with a national ID or family register is required to match:
  • Retiree ID card.
  • "Electronic" card of the retiree.
  • Definition of pension for beneficiaries of deceased retiree.
3-  In case of mismatching data, the ticket will be cancelled after deducting 35% of its value, and in case the customer wishes to travel, a ticket of full value will be issued outside the offer according to the availability of seats.
4- Choosing (agree) means agreeing to the terms and conditions.
  • I agree to the terms and conditions of the retirees offer.
